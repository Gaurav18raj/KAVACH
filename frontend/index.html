Project: KAVACH

Motive: To eradicate digital banking fraud (like the "Jamtara" screen-sharing & phishing scams) in India using Privacy-Preserving Behavioral AI. KAVACH validates who is typing, not just what they type, rendering stolen OTPs and passwords useless to attackers.
